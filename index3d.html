3D Tic-Tac-Toe Solver (3D UI)

How to Play

Objective: Get the specified number of pieces in a row (default: 3) in any direction to win.

  • Start: Set board dimensions and win length, then click "Start Game"
  • Make a Move: Click on any empty cell to place your piece (X or O)
  • Rotate Board: Click and drag the polyhedron near the top-right corner to rotate the 3D view
  • AI Move: Click "AI Move" to let the computer play for the current player
  • AI Depth: Higher values (1-6) make the AI stronger but slower
  • Undo: Take back the last move

Win Directions: You can win along any axis (X, Y, Z), any planar diagonal, or any 3D space diagonal.
